Hello and Welcome to the TU Freiberg Youtube Image grabber,
with the help of this tool you are able to read the script, subtitles or auto-generated subtitles from a selected video.

In order to start, you have to first find a video you like.

Once you have a video, you need the link of said video.
Lets take this one for example: Wormholes Explained - Breaking Spacetime by Kurzgesagt - In a Nutshell
with the youtube link: "https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9P6rdqiybaw"

Now that we have this link, please look at the end of the link, you can see the ID of your video.
This ID is needed to continue.
In this case: "9P6rdqiybaw"
The ID you now have, you can enter into the textbox seen on your screen.

Once you've entered the ID, you can click the button next to it to proceed to select a language you want to extract.
Confirm the language by pressing the OK button.
